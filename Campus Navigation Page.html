Navigating Stevens Campus

Upper Campus Vs. Lower Campus

Upper Campus

Upper campus holds many non-academic buildings such as residence halls, dining locations, and the library.

Lower Campus

Lower campus is mainly composed of academic buildings, so this is where you will have most of your classes. Lower campus also has most professors' offices, so this is where you may be going in order to go to office hours.
