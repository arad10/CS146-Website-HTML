What Is BittQuack All About?

Welcome to BittQuack, the number one source for all information you need to survive your first year at Stevens as a CS major. For all you incoming freshmen reading this, don't worry. This whole website is dedicated to making sure you excel here and know everything you need to know for your first year at Stevens. This website started as a project for what will be one of your freshmen classes, CS 146: Intro to Web Programming and Development.

Why is Computer Science important?

Computer Science has become important in everyone's daily lives. Can you imagine a world without the internet? Thanks to Computer Science, over the years, Computer Scientists cultivated the internet. Without the internet, everything we do now would be much more difficult like finding a source for that essay you have due tomorrow. Imagine having to handwrite every paper you turn in? You can't right? Thanks to Computer Science, all of the technology we have is consumer friendly and allows for people's lives to be changed by being able to perform tasks and desires much easier and faster. Additionally, through the creativity and opportunistic thinking of computer scientists, they have learned to manipulate code into making the daily lives of people easier. For example, Uber has made obtaining a taxi anywhere in the United States much easier than having to hassle with hailing a cab or dealing with a taxi service. Uber has even enabled "taxi-less" regions to have taxis available. Without Computer Science, Uber would cease to exist along with every piece of software you use. Computer Science both solves problems in all areas and fields to the daily lives of regular people. Computer Science has become the foundation for the advanced society we live in today. Computer Scienc is important because it works in solving problems and improving solutions to society's drastic problems.

Why Major In Computer Science at Stevens?

The demand for Computer Scientists have risen exponentially over the last two decades, and it is not without reason. Now, I won't bore you with a block of text but instead, a cool list of why you should major in Computer Science.

  • The freedom you have. As computer scientists, you can go into almost any field/company that suits you for a job
  • The high salary (refer to the salary table below for Stevens graduates specifically)
  • Your passion for techology! Computer Science is a heavily technological field that works with how a computer works and guides you into how to make your own programs, websites, etc.
